What are three primary responsibilities of a UIView?
-Displaying content on the screen
-Handling user interactions
-Managing layout and subviews

What does documentation call a view that's embedded in another view?
-Subview

What does documentation call the parent view that's embedding the other view?
-Superview

What is a view's frame?
-Defines its position and size relative to its superview's coordinate system

How is a view's bounds different from its frame?
-Frame is relative to the superview
-Bounds is relative to the view's own coordinate system
